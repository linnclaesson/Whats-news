Sampha announces new album "Lahai"

Published: October 5th, 1pm.

The new album LAHAI will be the second album release from the London artist and the first release since his debut album Process in 2017.

Sampha has recently made an exciting announcement regarding his music career. The London singer-songwriter took to Instagram to reveal the upcoming release of his highly anticipated new album, Lahai. This album serves as the follow-up to his successful 2017 project, Process. Sampha explained that the title of the album, Lahai, holds special significance as it is his grandfather's name, which also happens to be his middle name.

When the post of the announcement was made, the date of the release was unknown, however, we now know the official release date for Lahai is October 20th. Sampha shared his enthusiasm for this new musical chapter through his Instagram post. He described the themes and emotions that will be explored in the album, including fever dreams, continuums, dancing, generations, syncopation, bridges, grief, motherlands, love, spirit, fear, flesh, and flight.

In addition to this exciting news, Sampha has been giving fans a sneak peek into his musical journey. In august he shared a video clip of an unreleased and untitled song on his Instagram account, providing a taste of what's to come. Prior to this, in June, he made a comeback with his single, Spirit 2.0, his first solo single in six years. Furthermore, he has featured on Travis Scott's album Utopia, appearing on the track "My Eyes" alongside Bon Iver.

Stay tuned for more updates and details about Sampha's upcoming album, Lahai, as he continues to captivate audiences with his unique sound and heartfelt lyrics.
